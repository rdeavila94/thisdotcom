Lobby Chat

A small app utilizing the WebSocket protocol for real time communication among people in a lobby. Users select their username and a lobby to join, and are then connected to everyone else in that lobby. Users are able to send messages and their location. The UI was inspired by the iOS Messages app.

This.com

This website was built to 1) establish an online presence and 2) return to the fundamentals of front-end dev. I wrote every line of HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript; no use of frameworks or libraries, only compilation tooling (Babel + node-sass). I also wanted to minimize the amount of JavaScript and only use it when absolutely necessary (which turned out to be the scrolling fade animations). The artistic approach for the site was for it to be lighthearted, playful, and risky.
